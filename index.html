Women's Health
Menopause and Your Cycle

Although women make up half of the population, there remains a significant gap in resources and support for their health, particularly around menstruation and menopause. These topics are often considered taboo, leading to feelings of frustration, shame, isolation, and a lack of open dialogue.

The menstrual cycle is more than just a monthly occurrence, it’s a vital part of a woman’s health, influencing her energy, mood, metabolism, cognitive function, and reproductive ability throughout her life. Women experience their cycles for decades, and when menopause arrives, it marks another major transition, bringing lasting changes to the body and mind. Understanding and supporting these phases is essential for long-term health, balance, and well-being.
